I am a Research Scientist in Google Research Brain Team. I received my PhD in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics and MSc in Statistics at the University of Southern California. The focus of my research has been machine learning and statistical modeling in biological and medical problems, uncertainty estimation and out-of-distribution detection in deep learning.
